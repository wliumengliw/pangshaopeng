Shaopeng Pang is an Associate Professor and Master's Supervisor at the School of Electrical Engineering and Automation, Qilu University of Technology. He received his Ph.D. from Beihang University in 2018 and joined Qilu University of Technology in the same year. His work centers on complex systems control, neural networks, and data analytics.

Deputy Director, Institute of Intelligent Control and Simulation Algorithms
Teaching: MATLAB and Power System Simulation

Research

Research Directions

Three major themes define the current research profile.

Complex Systems Control

Controllability, robustness, and edge dynamics in complex and temporal networks.

Neural Networks

Learning-based modeling and intelligent recognition in engineering and interdisciplinary scenarios.

Data Analytics

Data-driven analysis, structured modeling, and decision support for complex systems.
